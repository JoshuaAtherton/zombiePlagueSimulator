Simulate a zombie plague

Description:

  • Black circles are healthy survivors
  • Red circles are infected zombies
  • Survivors have to gather food to remain alive. If they fail to gather food their health is decrease. If their health is less than or equal to zero they become a zombie.
  • Zombies will chase survivors to infect them. But there are also resistance pockets that have come up with a cure. Because of this their is a small chance that a zombie can become a survivor again, with the condition that they now only have 50 percent health.
  • Simulation starts with one infected zombie that infects other people and turns them into zombies when touched.
